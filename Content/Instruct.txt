pick up shield colors to destroy the 
shields around the asteroids and mines.
You can not destroy an asteroid or 
mine while you have a shield active.

Mouse controls(PlayerOne):
LMB - Move toward mouse.
RMB - Shoot toward mouse.
Middle Btn - Release shields.

Keyboard controls(PlayerTwo):
W/A/S/D - Move ship
Up/Down/Left/Right arrows - shoot in direction
Q - Release shields.
